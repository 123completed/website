Eyal Rubin, Founder, CEO

Eyal Rubin has been the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of 1 2 3 Completed since January 2009. Eyal brings more than 20 years of experience in enterprise application infrastructures and professional services. Eyal Co-founded Transparency software and served as VP Reaserch and Development and CTO of Transparency Software. Eyal Co-founded and served as CEO of 2Train4 INC (acquired by eWave). Eyal is a recognized visionary leader, entrepreneur, inventor, enterprise software leading consultant, a top Oracle & J2EE expert and instructor for leading edge technology companies. Eyal is an expert in managing technically complex projects, complex enterprise system architectures and enterprise monitoring systems for performance, security and compliance. Eyal established powerful partnerships and provided services and strategic technology consultancy to more than 100 accounts. As part of Eyal's customer and partner list you can find: Oracle, Mercury Interactive (acquired by HP) , Cisco Systems, Computer Associates, HP, BEA systems (acquired by Oracle), Sun Microsystems (acquired by Oracle), Amdocs (NYSE: DOX), Comverse Technologies and many others. Eyal earned his Computer Science and Economics Degree in Bar-Ilan University, Israel.

Alon brings more than 27 years of experience in the IT industries. Alon brings to 1 2 3 Completed years of management experience, system analysis capabilities, and experience in leading R&D teams, worldwide project leading and years of experience in system implementation.
